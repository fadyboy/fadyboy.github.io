Services

PROCUREMENT

BLOPROC INTERNATIONAL INC is a global player in international procurement. We source, procure and deliver equipments from OEM's ( ORIGINAL EQUIPMENT MANUFACTURERS) according to client specification in real time

We also specialise in PROJECT PROCUREMENT of equipment for prompt and timely delivery of projects. The various type of equipment we specialize in are:-

  • Mechanical Equipment
  • Electrical Equipment
  • Instrumentation Equipment
  • General office hardware
  • Oilfield tools and replacement spares

FREIGHT BROKERAGE

Bloproc International Inc is an efficient and safe means of connecting to transporters. We come in handy in freight brokerage by bringing together a shipper that needs to transport goods with an authorized motor carrier that wants to provide the service.

Bloproc will ensure safe and real time delivery of goods to the desired destination in very safe condition. At Bloproc we work with certified transport owners and safe drivers to ensure service delivery.
