About Us

BLOPROC INTERNATIONAL INCORPORATED is a procurement and logistics, freight brokerage company incorporated in Canada with OBLO ENERGY SERVICES NIG LTD as the Nigerian affiliate. Oblo Energy Services is a major procurement and logistics company, servicing the oil & gas sector in Nigeria.

BLOPROC INTERNATIONAL INC provides global expediting, procurement and logistics service, assisting clients to fulfil their requirements efficiently regardless of which part of the world the goods are being sourced.

OUR MISSION

To establish and maintain long lasting partnership with clients, through a better understanding of their needs and on target completion of our commitments to them: and to adopt a service delivery, solution-provider approach to our areas of expertise.

To satisfy our customers with prompt and efficient service delivery all the time
